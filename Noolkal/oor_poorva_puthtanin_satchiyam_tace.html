                    .               ,           .                        .

This Vedic and Vedantic imagery is foreign to our present mentality which does not believe in the living truth of the symbol, because the revealing imagination intimidated by the intellect has no longer the courage to accept, identify itself with and boldly embody a psychic and spiritual vision; but it is certainly very far from being a childish or a primitive and barbarous mysticism; this vivid, living, luminously poetic intuitive language is rather the natural expression of a highly evolved spiritual culture ( , 8 ) .

 “ thus in thee - it is not otherwise than thus - action cleaves not to a man ”      ‘  ’   ‘   ’   ‘  ’   ‘   ’                      ெ    .     ; .      ( , 19 ) .

                           .

                       ( , 365 ) .     :

  ,  ,   , “ ,      .     ”  .   “  ,   .       .      .      .        ”  .     . “ ,      .  ? ”  .  , “ ,   ? ”  .  “   ;   , “       ;    .   ,    ;         ” .      ”  .  ,
