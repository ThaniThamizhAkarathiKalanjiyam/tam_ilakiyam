## brahmi script
The Brahmi inscription found engraved on the outer surface of Black andRed-Ware with three horizontal parallel lines drawn along with four Brahmi lettersnamely ku, ru, vi and ka. The reading could be related with sufficient letter  ‘ n ’   ( d; )  sothe letters read together with  ‘ n ’   ( d; )  suffix may mean the name of the person as  ‘ un-ātn  ’ . The suffix letter in this is conjecturally added to become portion of script andmissing of the broken sherd. The proper noun of the Brahmi script is suggestive ofeither the quality of the individual as philanthropist similar to the nature of rain.
